We will be using the development environment, CodePen.

  • Share and export your pens.

CodePen - Forking a Pen

In CodePen, we will be sharing and editing our snippets in 'pens'. Forking another person's pen is simple:

  • Open up their pen.

  • Click the "Fork" button at the top of the page.
